About liftED

Our Mission

LiftED is dedicated to breaking down barriers to education in underserved communities across Kenya. Our mission is to provide digital tools, scholarships, and safe learning environments that empower children to overcome poverty and achieve their dreams. We believe every child deserves access to quality education regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Vision

We envision a kenya where every child has the opportunity to recieve quality education and build a brighter future.Through innovative digital learning solutions, community partnerships, and sustainable support system, we aim to create lasting change that lifts entirecommunities uot of poverty through education.

Our Core Values

  • Equity: We believe every child deserves equal access to quality education regardless of their socioeconomic background.
  • Innovation: We leverage technology and creative solutions to overcome traditional barriers to education.
  • Community: We work closely with local communities to ensure sustainable and culturally relevant educational programs.
  • Transparency: We maintain open communication about our programs, impact, and use of donor funds.
  • Empowerment: We focus on building confidence and leadership skills in students to create lasting change.

Our story

Founded in 2020, liftEDbegan as a grassroot initiative in the Kawangware slam of Nairobi, where we witnessed firsthand the transformative power of education. What started with just 10 students has grawn into a comprihesive program serving over 500 children across multiple communities in kenya . Our journey has been marked by the incredible success stories of students like E lizabeth, Regina and Kevin, who have overcome sighificant barriers to persue their dreams.

Our Programs

We provide comprehensive educational support through various programs designed to meet the unique needs of underserved communities:

  • Digital Learning Centers: Computer labs and internet access for students to access online educational resources.
  • Scholarship Programs: Financial support for school fees, uniforms, and learning materials.
  • Mentorship Programs: One-on-one guidance from successful professionals and university students.
  • Community Outreach: Educational workshops for parents and community leaders.
  • Leadership Development: Programs to build confidence and leadership skills in students.
