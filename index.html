About LiftED

Founded in 2020, LiftED began as a grassroots initiative in the Kawangware slum of Nairobi, where we witnessed firsthand the transformative power of education. What started with just 10 students has grown into a comprehensive program serving over 500 children across multiple underserved communities in Kenya.
